About Me

Every stroke is a decision.

I’m Bashir Yaqub, a pencil artist based in Nairobi. I work entirely by hand — no digital tools, no shortcuts. Just graphite on paper, built up layer by layer until the person in front of me comes alive on the page.

I love portraits most. There’s something powerful about capturing someone’s likeness so precisely that they feel it when they see it. That reaction — that silence — is what keeps me drawing.
